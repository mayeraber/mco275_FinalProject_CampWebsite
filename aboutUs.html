About Us

Camp Ohr Shraga was founded in 1965 by Rav Nesanel Quinn and Reb Zelik Epstein. It was the successor to Reb Shraga Feivel Mendlowitz’s Camp Mesivta, the first yeshiva summer camp, which operated in Ferndale from 1937 through the early 1960’s.Camp Ohr Shraga maintains the latest state of the art facilities to enhance their campers’ summers. The campus is equipped with acres of sports fields, an outdoor olympic-sized pool as well as a canteen. Our dynamic and experienced Rabbeim are carefully chosen in order to enhance the boys' summer learning and keep them up to par for the school year.
